How it works

Simple. Automatic.
Always there.

OK? — Safety & Wellness Alert works quietly in the background. You do one thing each day — it handles everything else.

✅

Daily Check-in

Every day at your chosen time, OK? — Safety & Wellness Alert sends a notification. Open the app and tap OK. Your guardian hears nothing — no news is good news.

🆘

Emergency Alert

Tap NOT OK to send your guardian an immediate email — with or without your GPS location. No phone call needed, no explanation required.

📧

Automatic Escalation

If you miss your check-ins beyond your configured threshold (1–7 days, your choice), OK? — Safety & Wellness Alert automatically emails your guardian — even if your phone is off.

🔔

Remind Me Later

Not ready right now? Tap Remind Me Later and OK? — Safety & Wellness Alert will prompt you again — 15 minutes to 2 hours later, your choice.
