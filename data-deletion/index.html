Your Privacy

Data Deletion

Effective date: February 5, 2026

📱

On-Device Data

OK? — Safety & Wellness Alert stores all app settings and check-in history locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data immediately.

✉️

Server-Side Logs

Alert delivery logs (timestamp, status, alert type — not your personal details) are retained for up to 90 days then automatically deleted. You can request early deletion at any time.

Delete Data Stored on Your Device

  • 1
    On your iPhone, press and hold the OK? — Safety & Wellness Alert app icon until a menu appears.
  • 2
    Tap "Remove App" from the menu.
  • 3
    Tap "Delete App" to confirm. This removes the app and all locally stored data from your device.

Request Server-Side Deletion Assistance

If you want help confirming removal or have a specific deletion request, email us at support@okcheck.app with the following details:

  • Subject: "OK? Data Deletion Request"
  • Your app profile email (only if you provided one in the app)
  • A brief description of what you want deleted

We may request minimal information to verify the request. We will process verified requests within a reasonable timeframe.
