### App Description (최대 4000자)

OK? is a simple, reliable daily wellness check-in app designed to keep your loved ones informed about your safety.

Every day at your chosen time, OK? sends you a notification asking one question: "Are you OK?" You simply tap OK to confirm you're safe, or tap NOT OK if you need help.

HOW IT WORKS

When you tap OK, your daily check-in is recorded. That's it — quick, easy, and done for the day.

When you tap NOT OK, you'll see options to alert your designated guardian by email. You can choose to include your current location or send without it. Your guardian receives an email immediately through our secure server.

If you miss your check-in for a number of days you've set (for example, 2 days), the app will automatically send an escalation alert email to your guardian when the app detects the missed check-ins — either when you next open the app or through periodic background checks. This helps ensure someone is notified even if you're unable to respond.

KEY FEATURES

- One-tap daily check-in with OK or NOT OK
- Configurable daily notification time and reminder interval
- Emergency email alert to up to 2 guardians
- Optional location sharing during emergencies only
- Automatic missed-days escalation alert to guardians
- Customizable alert message templates
- Check-in history log
- Multiple notification sound options
- All personal data stored locally on your device
- No account required, no tracking, no ads

PRIVACY FIRST

OK? is built with privacy at its core. Your personal information and check-in data are stored only on your device. Email alerts are sent through a secure server (HTTPS) only when triggered by you or by the missed-days safety feature. Location is shared only when you explicitly choose "Send with Location" during an emergency. We never track, collect, or sell your data.

IDEAL FOR

- Elderly individuals living alone
- Solo travelers
- People with medical conditions
- Anyone who wants a simple safety net for their loved ones

Stay safe. Stay connected. Are you OK?

### Review Notes (심사관 참고 사항 — 매우 중요)

Thank you for reviewing OK?. Here is a guide to help you test the app's core features:

SETUP (Required before testing):
1. Go to the Settings tab
2. Enter any name and email under "My Information" → tap "Save My Information"
3. Enter a valid email address under "Emergency Contact (Guardian)" → tap "Save Guardian Information"
4. The daily notification time and missed-days threshold can be left at their defaults

CORE FEATURE 1 — Manual Check-in:
- On the Check-in tab, tap "OK" to record a daily check-in
- The headline changes to "You checked in today ✓"
- The check-in appears in the History tab

CORE FEATURE 2 — Emergency Alert (Manual):
- Tap "NOT OK" on the Check-in tab
- You will see 4 options: Send with Location, Send without Location, Remind Me Later, Cancel/Ignore
- Tap "Send without Location" to send an emergency email to the configured guardian
- The email is sent via our secure HTTPS server (AWS API Gateway → Lambda → Amazon SES)
- Note: "Send with Location" requires location permission and shares a Google Maps link

CORE FEATURE 3 — Missed Days Escalation (Automatic):
- If a user misses check-ins beyond their configured threshold (default: 2 days), the app automatically sends an escalation alert email to the guardian
- This check runs when the app is opened and periodically in the background using BGAppRefreshTask
- A maximum of one escalation email is sent per calendar day to prevent duplicates
- The escalation email body is customizable via Settings → Safety Alert Preview

BACKGROUND MODES:
- This app uses Background fetch (BGAppRefreshTask) solely to check for missed check-in days and send escalation alerts to guardians. This is the app's core safety feature.

NOTIFICATIONS:
- Daily check-in reminders are scheduled as local notifications
- The app uses the Time Sensitive interruption level for safety-critical reminders
- Custom notification sounds (.caf files) are included in the bundle

NETWORK:
- All network communication uses HTTPS
- The only external endpoint is our AWS API Gateway for sending email alerts
- No user data is collected, tracked, or stored on any server

DATA STORAGE:
- All personal data (name, email, guardian info, check-in history) is stored locally on the device using UserDefaults
- No user account or sign-in is required
- No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs

LOCATION:
- Location permission (When In Use) is requested only when the user taps "Send with Location"
- Location is never stored, tracked, or sent automatically
- If the user has not granted location permission or has disabled the Auto-Location setting, the "Send with Location" button will show an appropriate message

DEMO EMAIL FOR TESTING:
- You may use any valid email as the guardian email to receive test alerts
- The sender address is: no-reply@okcheck.app (verified domain via Amazon SES)
