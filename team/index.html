Features

Everything your team needs.

Powerful safety tools wrapped in a simple, intuitive interface.

✅

Scheduled Check-ins

Set one or multiple daily check-in windows. Members receive reminders 5 minutes before and leaders are alerted if anyone misses their check-in.

🪟

Window-Based Tracking

Track check-ins by time window — see exactly who checked in during each scheduled slot and who is still missing, all at a glance.

🆘

SOS Emergency Alerts

One-tap SOS sends an immediate push notification and email to the team leader with the member's current GPS location. Available 24/7.

📊

Live Dashboard

Real-time overview: who's checked in, who's missing, and a complete activity timeline. Tap any card to drill into filtered member views.

💬

Team Messaging

Leaders broadcast announcements to the whole team or send direct messages to individual members. Members reply directly to their leader.

📥

Export Reports

Download check-in history and message records as a CSV for any date range. Open directly in Google Sheets for compliance and record-keeping.

📍

Privacy-First Location

Location is NEVER shared during normal check-ins. Only shared with the team leader when a member misses a check-in or triggers SOS.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Timely reminders before check-in, overdue alerts, and missed check-in notifications — all adjusted to each member's local timezone automatically.

🔒

Secure Login

Sign in with Apple, Google, or email. Face ID / Touch ID unlock supported for fast, secure access. Delete your account anytime from the app.

How it works

Simple for everyone.

Team leaders set the schedule. Members just tap.

1

Create a Team

Set up your team with a name and icon. Share the invite code with your members. They join in seconds.

2

Set Check-in Times

Configure daily and scheduled check-in times. Set how long to wait before marking someone as missed.

3

Members Check In

Members tap the check-in button when prompted. If someone misses, you get an instant alert with their last location.

🆘

SOS Anytime

In an emergency, any member can send SOS — instantly alerting the team leader with their current GPS location.
