Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-05

OK? is designed so that providing personal information is optional. The app can work with minimal data. If you choose to add information (such as your name, email, emergency contact email, or location), it is used only to provide app features you explicitly enable.

Information you may provide (optional)

  • Profile details: name, email, phone (if you choose to enter them)
  • Emergency contact details: contact name and email (if alerts are enabled)
  • Location: only if you enable location sharing for alerts
  • App settings: notification time, selected alert sound, and related preferences

How we use information

  • To schedule reminders and display your settings inside the app
  • To send an alert email to your chosen contact when you trigger or miss a check-in (only if enabled)
  • To include location in the alert message only when location sharing is enabled
  • To respond to support requests sent to support@okcheck.app

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. If you enable alert emails, the information required to send that email is processed solely to deliver the message.

Data storage

OK? stores your settings on your device. If you enable sending alert emails, the app transmits only the necessary content required to deliver the email.
