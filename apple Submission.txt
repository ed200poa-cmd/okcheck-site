## Description

OK? is a simple daily check-in app designed for personal safety and peace of mind.

Set a daily reminder to check in. If you don't respond — or if you actively select "NOT OK" — the app can send a pre-written alert email to a trusted contact (your guardian) to let them know you may need help.

HOW IT WORKS:
• Set your daily check-in time (e.g., 8:00 AM)
• When the reminder arrives, tap "OK" to confirm you're fine
• If something is wrong, tap "NOT OK" to alert your guardian
• If you miss your check-in, an automatic escalation alert can be sent

KEY FEATURES:
• Daily notification reminders with customizable time and sound
• One-tap check-in: OK or NOT OK
• Emergency guardian alerts via email
• Optional location sharing during emergencies (opt-in only)
• Support for up to 2 emergency contacts (guardians)
• Check-in history log
• Customizable escalation and re-prompt intervals
• Safety alert message preview and editing
• All data stored locally on your device — no account required
• No ads, no tracking, no social features

WHO IT'S FOR:
• People living alone who want a simple safety net
• Elderly individuals and their family members
• Anyone who wants daily wellness accountability
• Caregivers looking for a lightweight check-in tool

Your privacy matters. OK? works with minimal data. Personal information is optional, stored only on your device, and never shared except when you explicitly send an alert.

For support: support@okcheck.app

### Notes for Review

This app provides daily safety check-in reminders with optional emergency email alerts.

Key points for review:

1. NO ACCOUNT REQUIRED: The app works entirely without user registration. All data is stored locally on the device.

2. EMAIL ALERTS (optional feature): If the user configures a guardian email in Settings, the app can send emergency alert emails via our server when the user taps "NOT OK" or misses a check-in. This feature requires the user to manually enter guardian contact details.

3. LOCATION PERMISSION: Location is requested ONLY if the user enables "Auto-Location (Opt-in)" in Settings AND triggers an emergency alert. Location is never collected in the background or for any other purpose.

4. NOTIFICATIONS: The app uses local notifications for daily check-in reminders. Notification time and sound are configurable in Settings.

5. NO IN-APP PURCHASES, NO ADS, NO TRACKING.

To test the app:
- Open the app and tap "OK" to check in
- Tap "NOT OK" to see the Help Options screen (you can tap "Cancel / Ignore" without sending any email)
- Go to Settings to see notification, guardian, and alert configuration options
- Go to History to see past check-in records

No demo account is needed.
