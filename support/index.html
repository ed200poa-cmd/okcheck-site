Common Checks

Before reaching out, these steps resolve most notification issues:

  • 1
    iOS Settings → Notifications → OK?
    Confirm Allow Notifications, Sounds, and Time Sensitive are all enabled.
  • 2
    Focus / Do Not Disturb modes
    These modes may silence notifications from all apps, including OK? — Safety & Wellness Alert.
  • 3
    Device volume & silent switch
    Check that your device volume is turned up and the physical silent switch on the side of your iPhone is not engaged.
